Web Summit Global Tech Conference

Web Summit is a global conference focused on the engineering of Android applications. Web Summit provides a forum for developers to network with other developers, share techniques, announce apps and products, and to learn and teach. This two-day developer focused gathering will be held in Nairobi Kenya on August 6th to 8th 2020 and will be the largest of its kind in Africa.
